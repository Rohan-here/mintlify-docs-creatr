If you purchased a domain from Alibaba Cloud and want to use it on a Creatr project without moving the hosting to Creatr, then this guide will help you manually connect your Alibaba Cloud domain to Creatr.

### Step 1: Connect your domain to Creatr

1. From your Project workspace, go to **Settings** > **Manage Custom Domains**.
2. Enter **domain**.
3. Click **Save**

### Step 2: Log in to your Alibaba Cloud account

1. Open a new browser tab and log in to your **Alibaba Cloud account**.
2. Click **Console**.
3. Click **Domain names**.
![Alibaba DNS management interface showing Account Settings and Domains section](/images/custom-domain/alibaba/image.png)

### Step 3: Add the CNAME records

1. Click **Domain Name Resolution**.
2. Click **Add DNS Record**.
3. For **Record Type**, select **CNAME**.
4. In **Hostname**, enter **_acme-challenge.hostname**.
5. In **DNS Request Source**, select **Default**.
6. In **Record Value**, enter **hostname.a717d819c68725fc.dcv.cloudflare.com**.
7. In **TTL Period**, select **10 (Recommended)**.
8. Click **OK**.
![Alibaba DNS management interface showing Account Settings and Domains section](/images/custom-domain/alibaba/image_cname.png)

### Step 4: Add another CNAME record

1. Click **Add DNS Record** again.
2. For **Record Type**, select **CNAME**.
3. In the **Hostname** field, type **www**.
4. In **DNS Request Source**, select **Default**.
5. In **Record Value**, enter **customers.getcreatr.app**.
6. In **TTL Period**, select **10 (Recommended)**.
7. Click **OK**.

### Step 5: Verify connection

1. From your Creatr admin, go to **Settings** > **Manage Custom Domains**.
2. After your domain is verified, it will be displayed as connected on the **Manage Custom Domains** tab in your Project Settings.

> **Note**
>
> It's normal to have a warning stating, `Domain connection Pending`, because some providers can take longer to verify the connection.

## Troubleshooting

If the message **This domain is already connected is displayed**, then you need to **verify your domain and projects**.
